Differentiator · AI Risk Lens

Know who you're paying — before you pay.

Before you commit to a Pact, PACTA reads your counterparty's real on-chain history — completed vs. refunded deals, volume, track record — and turns it into a plain-language risk read with suggested milestones.

The numbers are computed deterministically on-chain. The AI only translates correct numbers into human language — it never invents data.

Differentiator · Linked identity

Verify once. Use any of your wallets.

  • Verify your identity once, then link your other wallets by signing with them.
  • Any linked wallet counts as the same verified you — no re-doing KYC.
  • Linking is never silent: a new wallet must prove it's yours, so no stranger can inherit your status.

Off-chain, minimal personal data, never holds funds, erasable on request.

The edge · reliable anywhere

One tap. Zero network. Nothing to break.

Demo mode runs the whole app on seeded data with simulated transactions and no network at all — a bulletproof live demo, and a proof-of-concept for a fully offline-first experience.

Honest framing: real settlement is always on Stellar. The offline outbox defers and completes on reconnect; demo mode is a presentation layer. The low-connectivity value is real.

Architecture

Non-custodial, and built to scale.

  • The contract is the backend. A deployed Soroban smart contract governs protection — PACTA holds nothing.
  • One clean seam. Every screen depends only on a ChainAdapter interface — never the Stellar SDK. A future EVM chain is one new adapter, zero screen changes.
  • Proven. That same seam is how demo mode swaps in a mock adapter — the multi-chain story isn't hand-waving.
