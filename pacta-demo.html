Interactive demo · Payment & Consumer

PACTA

Simplify every payment. Protect the ones that matter. A non-custodial wallet on Stellar that keeps working when the signal drops.

  1. Explore the wallet: balance, Send, Receive, Convert.
  2. Tap Online in the app header to go Offline.
  3. Send a payment — it gets queued, not lost.
  4. Tap Offline to reconnect — watch it auto-deliver.
  5. Open Pacts to release a protected payment.

Everything here is a self-contained, seeded demo — no network, no accounts. This is what a low-connectivity user experiences.
